HelpCare

Small acts of kindness can change a life.

Helping communities stay strong with fast, trusted support.

HelpCare is a local support network for families, seniors, and people affected by emergencies. We connect volunteers, services, and resources so everyone can get the help they need quickly.

  • Simple, clear help options for families and neighbors
  • Community support for food, medical care, and shelter
  • Easy contact details and fast response information

How HelpCare Works

Submit a request, choose the right service, and receive assistance from nearby volunteers and care partners.

Who We Help

Families in crisis, elderly neighbors, survivors of disasters, and anyone looking for compassionate community support.

Why HelpCare Matters

Our platform brings neighbors together, builds trust, and makes sure no one is left behind when help is needed most.

  • Fast response for urgent needs
  • Trusted local volunteer network
  • Support for food, medical care, and housing

Food packages and fresh produce are available to anyone in need.

Available every Tuesday from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM at the community center. Bring a valid ID if possible. We provide rice, canned goods, fresh vegetables, and ready-to-eat meals.

Contact: 0912-345-6789

Inquire: Ask the HelpCare desk or call the pantry coordinator for assistance.

Basic health consultation and guidance from licensed volunteers.

Health screenings are held every Sunday morning. Priority is given to seniors, pregnant women, and families with children.

Contact: 0998-765-4321

Inquire: Call the medical team to reserve a check-up slot.

A safe place to stay for families and individuals during emergencies.

Shelter is offered after fires, floods, or other urgent needs. HelpCare volunteers can arrange transport and basic supplies.

Contact: help@care.org

Inquire: Contact the shelter coordinator to request a safe space.

After-school help for children and students with school materials.

Volunteer tutors offer free support in reading, writing, and basic homework assistance. Sessions are held every Wednesday afternoon.

Contact: 0918-222-3344

Inquire: Reach out to the tutor team for learning session schedules.

Emotional and crisis support via phone for anyone who needs someone to talk to.

Available every day from 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM. Our volunteers offer compassionate listening and practical guidance.

Contact: 0917-555-8899

Inquire: Call the hotline to connect with a counselor immediately.

Free rides for medical appointments and community services.

HelpCare volunteers provide transportation for residents who cannot easily get to appointments or support centers.

Contact: 0912-788-1122

Inquire: Call to arrange pickup for your next appointment.
